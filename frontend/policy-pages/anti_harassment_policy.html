Anti-Harassment Policy

1. Commitment to a Safe Workplace

We are dedicated to maintaining a work environment that is free from harassment. Harassment of any kind, whether physical, verbal, or visual, will not be tolerated.

2. Definition of Harassment

Harassment includes unwelcome conduct that creates an intimidating, hostile, or offensive work environment. This includes, but is not limited to, slurs, jokes, intimidation, inappropriate physical contact, or unwanted sexual advances.

3. Reporting Procedures

If you experience or witness harassment, you are strongly encouraged to report it to Human Resources, your supervisor, or any member of the management team immediately. All reports will be handled confidentially and promptly investigated.

4. Resolution and Disciplinary Action

If an investigation confirms that harassment has occurred, the company will take appropriate disciplinary action against the offender, which may include termination of employment.
