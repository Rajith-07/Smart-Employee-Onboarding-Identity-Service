Data Privacy & Security Policy

1. Objective

This policy establishes the rules and procedures for protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our company's data and information systems.

2. Data Handling and Classification

All data must be classified according to its sensitivity (e.g., Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted). Employees must handle data appropriately based on its classification to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

3. Access Control

Access to sensitive data and systems is granted on a "need-to-know" and "least privilege" basis. Employees must not share their passwords or provide access to unauthorized individuals.

4. Security Incident Reporting

Any suspected or actual security breach, data loss, or unauthorized access must be reported immediately to the IT Security team. Prompt reporting is crucial for mitigating potential damage.
