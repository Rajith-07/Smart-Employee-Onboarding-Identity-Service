Code of Conduct

1. Purpose

Our company is committed to providing a safe, inclusive, and professional environment for everyone. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all employees, contractors, and partners.

2. Respect and Inclusivity

We treat everyone with dignity and respect. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated. We embrace diversity and strive to create a workplace where everyone feels they belong.

3. Professional Integrity

We act with honesty and integrity in all our dealings. We avoid conflicts of interest, protect confidential information, and comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

4. Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of this code, please report it immediately to HR or your manager. We strictly prohibit retaliation against anyone who makes a good faith report.
