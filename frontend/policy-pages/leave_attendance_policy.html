Leave & Attendance Policy

1. Working Hours

Our standard working hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Regular attendance and punctuality are expected from all employees.

2. Types of Leave

Employees are entitled to the following types of leave:

Leave Type Annual Allowance
Paid Time Off (PTO) 20 Days
Sick Leave 10 Days
Public Holidays As per regional calendar

3. Requesting Leave

All planned leave must be requested through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance and approved by your manager. In case of unexpected illness, please notify your manager as soon as possible.

4. Unpaid Leave

Unpaid leave may be granted at the discretion of management for extraordinary circumstances once all PTO is exhausted.
